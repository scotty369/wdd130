Rafting Website Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this rafting website is to establish an online presence for our rafting business. It will provide information about our rafting trips, services, and safety measures to attract adventure enthusiasts and promote our brand.

Audience

The target audience for the rafting website includes adventure seekers, families looking for outdoor activities, and corporate groups seeking team-building experiences. Three scenarios are:

  • Adventure Enthusiast: John is looking for thrilling outdoor activities to satisfy his need for adventure.
  • Family Vacation: The Smith family wants to find a safe yet exciting outdoor activity for their next vacation.
  • Corporate Team Building: A company is planning an outdoor team-building event for its employees.
